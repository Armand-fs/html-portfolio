Cinematic Gems: Armand's Choices

My Triad of Timeless Movies

The Hunger Games

In a dystopian future, young tributes from 12 districts are selected to participate
in a televised death match called the Hunger Games.



The Twilight Saga

A teenage girl falls in love with a vampire, leading to a series of romantic and dangerous
adventures involving vampires, werewolves, and human relationships.



Saving Private Ryan

Following the Normandy Landings during World War II, a group of U.S. soldiers go behind enemy
lines to retrieve a paratrooper whose brothers have been killed in action.
